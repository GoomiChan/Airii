Airii

Add in world dropship timetables to FireFall

Overview: What does it do? It places billboards at each of the drop ship landing pads telling you what ships are due to arrive at that that pad, what route they are taking and an estimate of how long it will take.

The panel opens as you approach it, names in green are friendly outpost while ones in red are hostile. The left most name is the next destination for the drop ship. As you walk away the panel will close itself again :)
